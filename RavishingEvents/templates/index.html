Services We Provide



Entertainment & Artist Management

Be it a comic's hilarious antics, a stomach- hurting stand-up act or an enchanting sufi night, don't let your dose of entertainment take a backseat amidst all the hoopla and shenanigans.

Wedding Creative

A dash of innovation can turn things around in ways more than one. We certainly cannot emphasize our preference enough for the unique and unusual. Trust us with your wedding design and creatives among some funky wedding ideas and concepts, and we promise you a love affair with our ideas.


Photography & Cinematography

It's not just enough to create a reel of soft- focus memories in your mind while your once-in-a-life, high-on-adrenaline moments unfold amidst myriad hues. We help you zoom in and capture some visual treasures through the most focused lens.

Food & Beverages

It is no secret that a gourmandizing platter and glasses oozing palate-satisfying beverages form the highlight of any Indian wedding. In keeping with the spirit of the occasion, we bet to delight your taste buds with the best catering deals only to make the experience more appetizing. Hungry, already?

Ancillary Services

Putting wedding events, successfully, together requires much more than just having the essentials in place. We, at Ravishing Events, provide a host of services, which include designing and styling wedding dresses, customizing wedding invitations and providing personalized communication among others.

Hospitality

Whoever claims to not enjoy a warm wedding reception and a fair bit of indulgence aces the lie game! Well, all we are trying to say is that you can never go wrong with showering your guests with some privileges and exclusivity. And we just help you ensure that your guests get a supreme taste of the grandeur that they are meant to experience.


Wedding Gifts & Hampers

A box of exotic cookies for a bridal shower or a hamper with assorted goodies for the wedding can be beautiful way to show love to your guests. Let us help you pick the choicest contents and add raw wedding fuzziness to those fine, delightful tokens.

Wedding Communications

How about letting every guest in on the smallest of details about your plans for your elaborate marriage celebrations without making any frenzied, last-minute goof-ups? Having an effective communication plan in place plays a crucial role in making your dream of bringing a fairy tale to life


Decor
